Military Spending Analysis

Military spending in absolute value

This bar plot shows the total military spending of the 10 countries.
  • The United States dominiates other countries over the total military spending.
  • China is in second place, in which military spending is twice as much spending as in the rest of the countries.

    •  

    Per capita military spending compare to per capita GDP

    According to the per capita bubble chart, there is a positive correlation between the per capita military spending and per capita GDP. The bubble size shows the population size of the country.
  • China per capita data is low due to large population.
  • The United States is the third most populous country, but it's per capita data is still the highest.

    •  

    Military spending growth(2011-2016)

    Military spending growth in absolute value and in percentage are shown on the same plot.
  • Only China and India have a positive growth.
  • The United States has significant negative growth in absolute value, but when refer to the percentage, it is only -16%, a decrease that is smaller than most of the countries where the growth is negative.
  • Brazil experienced the largest percentage decrease in military spending, about -34%.
