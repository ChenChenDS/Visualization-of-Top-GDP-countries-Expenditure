Education Spending Analysis

Education spending in absolute value

This bar plot illustrates the total healthcare spending in the 10 countries.
  • The United States dominates other countries over education spending throughout the sample period.
  • Total education spending keep rising for most of the years in the United States, China and India, though the increases are relatively small in India.
  • In Japan, the total education spending has been decreased significantly since 2012, though there is a slight rebound in 2016.

    •  

    Per capita dducation spending compare to per capita GDP

    This graph shows per capita education spending in comparison with per capita GDP of the six years.
  • Again, the United States still ranked first.
  • Canada's per capita is at the same level of the United State.
  • Germany, France and UK following closely.

    •  

    Education spending growth (2011-2016)

    Education spending growth in absolute value and in percentage are shown on the same plot.
  • China, as a fast-growing emerging market economy, grow at the fastest speed in educational development measured by both absolute values of growth and growth rate.
  • The biggest loser in education expansion during 2011-2016 is Japan. Its total education spending shrinks by more than 20% by the end of 2016.
  • Followed by Brazil, where the growth rate is -19%.
